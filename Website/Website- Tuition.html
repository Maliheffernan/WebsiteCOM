MY VISION

Tonic Tuition aspires to provide the highest quality education to students of all ages, in a safe and supportive environment.

Aiming to inspire all students to embrace their creativity and enrich their lives with diverse musical cultures.

MY MISSION

My aims as a music teacher are: 

• To give students a safe and non-judgemental space to be creative and express themselves. 

• To promote creativity and pass on the knowledge and tools for my students to become competent musical performers. 

• To motivate students to involve themselves in the rich musical culture and create opportunities to play with others and enhance their social interactions. 

• To improve my student’s confidence with encouragement and support; and inspire them to use this creative platform as a device to bring them joy.
